Welcome to The Commons Church

We are a family. What holds us together is not ethnicity, education, income, or politics. We are a family because of Jesus and owe Jesus a common allegiance. Christ, his broken body and shed blood, are at the center of all we do together.

We each come — bringing our unique stories — to journey life together as a community. It is not about uniformity, but unity, so that together we may live for something bigger than ourselves.

So no matter where you are in life we invite you to come as you are and encourage you to live for something greater.

— From Pastors Seth & Jon

FBCNC and Our Unique Partnership

Those of us from FBCNC share 130 years of stories of this community and have an embodied multiethnic church life. The journey for The Commons Church began just 7 years ago in North Park and we carry over our work in connecting dechurched young people to the story of Jesus. Together, we are two churches in the pursuit of one common vision and purpose: the Kingdom of God at work here in South San Diego.

We often hear today religious tribalism and us vs. them faith, Christianity that cares more about a big house than a big heart, and churches that want to stay the same because that's what feels comfortable. This is a unique adventure. We are on the front lines of the Spirit's work in our culture. God is moving us to partner, to reconcile, to get out of our comfort zones, and to give up the so-called good life for that which is truly life. This journey we are on is not easy. We must forego status and power. We have to collaborate instead of compete. We need to mutually submit to people unlike us. Yet the Spirit empowers us on this path and propels towards this end.
